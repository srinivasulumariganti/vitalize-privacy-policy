Privacy Policy – VITalize

VITalize respects user privacy and is committed to protecting it.

Information Collection

VITalize does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

Login Feature

The login feature uses a fixed username and password only to allow access to app features. No login information is stored, transmitted, or linked to any server or database.

News Content

News articles are fetched from Google News RSS feeds. Full articles are displayed using an in-app WebView or opened in the Chrome browser. Any advertisements shown belong to the original news websites and are not controlled by VITalize.

Local Storage

The daily streak feature is stored locally on the user’s device and is not shared externally.

Third-Party Services

VITalize does not use AdMob, analytics, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

VITalize does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13.
